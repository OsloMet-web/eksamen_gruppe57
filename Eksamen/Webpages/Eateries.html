Eateries and Cafes
OsloMet Pilestredet Campus

There are 6 different Eateries and Cafes located at the educational institution.
Welcome for a good cup of coffee, lunch or dinner!

Olaf Spiseri

Pilestredet 35, 0166 Oslo

Olaf Spiseri offers a buffet with hot food, soups, cold cuts and bread, where you pay by weight. You will also find a wide selection of wraps and buttered products in addition to cakes and various hot and cold drinks.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:30

Anna's Spiseri

Pilestredet 52, 0167 Oslo

Anna's Spiseri is the largest eatery on the OsloMet Pilestredet Campus. Here we offer hot food, salad, cold cuts and bread by weight. We also have a wide selection of products, drinks, baked goods and dairy products.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday 08:00 - 17:00

Andrea's Spiseri

Pilestredet 32, 0166 Oslo

In Andrea's dining room, you can find hot food, soup, salad, cold cuts and bread from our buffet, and pay by weight. In addition, a wide selection of products, baked goods and good coffee drinks.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:30

Kafe Eva

Pilestredet 48, 0167 Oslo

Kafe Eva has a large selection of kiosk items, prepackaged salads and baguettes that you can take with you. The café also offers food such as toast or soup, which can be heated in the café.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:00

Kafe Årstiden

Stensberggata 26 - 28, 0170 Oslo

At Kafe Årstiden, both the menu and the interior are adapted to the season in terms of ingredients, flavors and colours. Here you get seasonal hot dishes and salad by weight. The season also has its own yoghurt bar, and offers freshly buttered baguettes, cakes and a wide selection of coffee drinks.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday 08:00 - 14:00

Union167 Fyrhuset

Pilestredet 52, 0167 Oslo

Union Fyrhuset at OsloMet offers freshly baked baked goods, sandwiches, fresh croissants and buns straight from the oven. Try our delicious stone oven baked Italian pizza made by our pizzaiolo Paolo which we think is the best pizza in town. We serve beer, wine and a large selection of tea and coffee drinks.

Opening Hours:

Monday - Friday 10:00 - 18:00
