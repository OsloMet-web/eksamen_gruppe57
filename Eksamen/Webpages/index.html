Pilestredet Campus

OsloMet - Storbyuniversitetet is a recognized university located in the heart of Norway's vibrant capital, Oslo. With a strong commitment to research, teaching and community service, OsloMet has developed into one of Norway's leading institutions for higher education. The university's vision is to "meet the challenges of the future" through innovation, inclusion and knowledge dissemination.

OsloMet's largest Campus is located in the center of Oslo in Pilestredet. The campus has its own unique character and specialisation, and offers a wide range of subject areas and study programmes. You can find Pilestred Campus here:

At OsloMet Pilestredet you will find a diverse and international environment, with students and staff from all over the world. The university places great emphasis on practice teaching and collaboration with business and society, which gives students practical skills and experiences that are relevant to working life. With modern teaching facilities, libraries, laboratories and student services that support students' academic and personal development, OsloMet has a strong commitment to giving students a meaningful educational experience.

OsloMet is also known for its research in areas such as health, technology, pedagogy, social sciences and more. The university plays an important role in driving innovation and knowledge development in Norway. Whether you are interested in pursuing a bachelor's degree, master's degree or doctorate, or simply want to explore exciting research opportunities, OsloMet has a lot to offer. Pilestredet Campus provides a vibrant learning environment, and the university's commitment to excellent teaching and research makes it an ideal place to pursue your educational goals.
