Eateries and cafes on campus

Olaf Spiseri

Pilestredet 35, 0166 Oslo

Olaf spiseri offers a buffet with hot food, soups, cold cuts and bread, where you pay by weight. You will also find a wide selection of wraps and buttered products in addition to cakes and various hot and cold drinks.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:30

Anna's Spiseri

Pilestredet 52, 0167 Oslo

Anna's Spiseri is our largest eatery on the OsloMet Pilestredet Campus. Here we offer hot food, salad, cold cuts and bread by weight. We also have a wide selection of wraps and buttered products, cold and hot drinks, baked goods and dairy products.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:00 - 17:00

Andrea's Spiseri

Pilestredet 32, 0166 Oslo

In Andrea's dining room, you can help yourself to hot food, soup, salad, cold cuts and bread from our buffet, and pay by weight. In addition, you will find a wide selection of wraps and buttered products, tempting cakes and baked goods and good coffee drinks. Everything served in large, bright and modern premises.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:30

Kafe Eva

Pilestredet 48, 0167 Oslo

Kafe Eva has a large selection of kiosk items, prepackaged salads and baguettes that you can take with you. The café also offers food such as toast or soup, which can be heated in the café.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:00

Kafe Årstiden

Stensberggata 26 - 28, 0170 Oslo

At Kafe Årstiden, both the menu and the interior are adapted to the season in terms of ingredients, flavors and colours. Here you get seasonal hot dishes and salad by weight. The season also has its own yoghurt bar, and offers freshly buttered baguettes, cakes and a wide selection of coffee drinks.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:00 - 14:00

Kaffelars Kaffebar

Pilestredet 46, 0167 Oslo

Take a break between lecture and independent reading, enjoy a good cup of coffee, some tea, spread food and maybe a piece of cake.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:00 - 15:00

Lillefri Kaffebar

Pilestredet 42, 0167 Oslo

In the coffee shop Lillefri in Bislett, you get good coffee drinks and tea, delicious cakes and freshly made wraps and baguettes.

Opening Hours : Monday - Friday 08:30 - 12:30
