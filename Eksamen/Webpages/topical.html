The Interplay between Technology and Society

Todays society relies on the internet and with the satellite internet technology more people across the world can have access to a “larger part” of the world. The case is relevant to society, technology and our future profession in a way that we bear responsibility with future innovations, research and how we use technology, which Anders Braarud Hanssen introduce in module 1.4 (RRI). It is therefore important to look at how previous and current innovations can or will affect our society to prevent unintended consequences.
